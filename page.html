Welcome to Westcott Street Fair!

For over 30 years, volunteers have planned, operated, and made the Westcott Street Cultural Fair a vibrant and diverse event. The Fair features some of the best performers, food, and vendors that the Westcott neighborhood and surrounding area has to offer. Join us for a day of fun, food, and festivities!

What's New?

This year’s fair will feature an expanded food court with delicious cuisines from around the world! We’re thrilled to welcome surprise performances from local artists. Bring the little ones for face painting, crafts, and interactive games in our Kids’ Zone! Join us in celebrating 30 years of the Westcott Street Cultural Fair—rain or shine!
